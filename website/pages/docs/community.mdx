# Community

Arcon is as of now a research project with features driven by us at the [CDA](https://cda-group.github.io/) group. There are however plans to 
build a community around Arcon in the future when the project reaches a more stable position. 

## Reach out

If you find Arcon interesting and want to initiate a collaboration or contribute to the project, reach out on 
Github or by email ``mmeldrum@kth.se``.
